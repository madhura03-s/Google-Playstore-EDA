# Google Play Store — EDA & Feature Engineering

## Problem Statement
Today, 1.85 million different apps are available for users to download. 
Android users have even more to choose from, with 2.56 million available 
through the Google Play Store.

**Objective:** Find the most popular category, app with the largest number 
of installs, highest rated apps and more.

---

## Dataset
- **Records:** 10,841 rows × 20 columns
- `googleplaystore.csv` — Original raw dataset
- `google_cleaned.csv` — Cleaned dataset (generated by notebook)

---

## Steps Covered

### 1. Data Cleaning
- Removed invalid row in Reviews column (value 3.0M)
- Converted Reviews to integer
- Cleaned Size column — replaced M with 000, removed k, handled Varies with device
- Cleaned Installs and Price — removed special characters +, ,, $
- Converted Last Updated to datetime and extracted Day, Month, Year columns

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Removed 1181 duplicate app records
- Univariate analysis of numerical and categorical features
- KDE plots for numerical features
- Count plots for categorical features (Type, Content Rating)
- Pie chart and bar chart for top app categories

### 3. Feature Engineering
- Extracted Day, Month, Year from Last Updated column

## Key Insights
- Family category has the most apps — 18% of total
- Games category has the highest installs — 35 Billion+
- 271 apps have a perfect 5.0 rating
- Most popular game: **Subway Surfers**
- Most popular communication app: **Hangouts**
- Most popular productivity app: **Google Drive**
- Most popular social app: **Instagram**
